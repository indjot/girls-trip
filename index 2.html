Pedal Boats & Picnic

Regent's Park
10:00 AM
~1–2 hrs
£9 off-peak (11am–1pm)
About

Hire a pedal boat on the lake for a lazy loop around the water, then find a spot on the grass to lay out a picnic. A relaxed, low-effort stretch to open the day.

Address

The Regent's Park Boathouse, Hanover Gate, London NW1 4NU

Good to know
  • Boat hire is usually walk-up, but can queue on sunny weekends
  • Nearest tube: Great Portland Street or Baker Street
  • Grab a spot near the boathouse if it starts to rain

Cable Cars at The O2

North Greenwich · Emirates Air Line
4:00 PM
~30–45 min
£7 single · £13.50 return
About

The Emirates Air Line cable car glides across the Thames between North Greenwich and the Royal Docks, giving sweeping views over the O2, Canary Wharf, and the river below — a scenic way to close out the day.

Address

Emirates Air Line, Edmund Halley Way, London SE10 0FR

Good to know
  • Book ahead on busy days to skip the queue
  • Contactless / Oyster works the same as the tube
  • Best light for photos is right before sunset
